FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is cosmopolitan???
Ans. It is the name of our fest which beholds the objective of promoting an assortment of cultures of various parts of India along with some foreign cultures. Briefly it is the improvised version of Multi-ethnic festival.
2. What is the motive behind the fest and what kind of performances are included in it??
Ans. Motive behind the fest is to introduce students with the blend of our Indian & Foreign cultures and to congregate a wider scope of knowledge about our cultural diversity. Performance like regional dances (e.g.-Bihu, Kathakali, Odishi etc.), traditional music, and dramas will be performed.
3. Who can participate in this fest?
Ans. Only students of NIT Rourkela, irrespective of their branch and course pursued are allowed to participate in this fest..
4. How to participate in this fest?
Ans. A link will be sent to each and every student in webmail account. And on clicking on the link, the student will be directed to the google form of Cosmopolitan where the students are required to fill up their necessary details regarding the fest.
5. Is there any participation fee to perform I this festival?
Ans. No participation fee is required to perform in this fest. Our primary focus is to outshine your cultural talents.
6. Will there be anyone to guide the performers to perform in a better way?
Ans. Yes, choreographers will be appointed according to the regional performances and the performances will be under supervision of the choreographer.
7. Will the costumes and required instruments will be provided by the college?
Ans. All kinds of costumes, instruments and other requirements related to the performances will be provided by the college.
8. Will any certificates or awards be given to performers?
Ans. Each and every performer will be given a certificate of participation.
9. Is there any restriction to modern dance forms?
Ans. Yes, modern dances are strictly interdicted to be performed.
10. Whom should I contact for instant response?
Ans. You can contact with our convener N. Vivek Kumar (8984742100).
